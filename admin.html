🎬 Why YouTube for Videos? (Zero Lag!)

✅ YouTube serves videos from Google's global CDN — zero buffering

✅ Videos auto-compress for mobile — saves your hosting space

✅ Works on any internet speed — adaptive quality

✅ How to get a YouTube Video ID: Go to your video → Copy the part after v= in the URL

Example: youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ → ID is dQw4w9WgXcQ
